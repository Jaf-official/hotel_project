About The Confluence

Welcome to Confluence Hotel, where elegance meets comfort in the heart of Lokoja in Kogi State. Our boutique hotel offers a unique blend of modern amenities and warm hospitality, making it the ideal destination for both leisure and business travelers.

At The CONFLUENCE, we prioritize your comfort and convenience. Our attentive staff is always ready to assist you with any requests, ensuring a seamless and memorable stay.
