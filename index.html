The History Of Our Hotel

Welcome to "The CONFLUENCE" - Where Timeless Elegance Meets Modern Luxury! Established in the heart of a bustling city in 1991, The CONFLUENCE was born out of a visionary dream to create comfort for discerning travelers and tourists. The hotel quickly became a favorite among the city's elite for its warm hospitality and refined ambiance. Over the years, The CONFLUENCE has evolved into a landmark destination, meticulously preserving its rich heritage while embracing contemporary comforts.
